Four tracks

Three ways to tell one corten fire at Villa Sjövik.

Track 01

When it's cold and dark

"The Quaruba through a Nordic autumn evening"

A corten fire is at its best exactly when the terrace is cold and the lake has gone black — which, this far north, is most of the year. I'd let the Quaruba do the one thing summer photography never shows: the glow that pulls people back outside in October, glass on every side, forty metres from the water. It reframes an outdoor fireplace from a July luxury into the reason a garden stays used all year — and that is the season most of your buyers never realised they were shopping for.

Off-season · The glow · Year-round desire

Track 02

The Tuesday fire

"The Piquia as an everyday habit, not an event"

The Piquia is compact enough to become a habit — the fire you light on an ordinary evening, not only when guests arrive. I'd show it exactly that way: small, unfussy, already lit before the coffee's gone cold. That moves it out of the "special-occasion splurge" bracket and into "I'd use this every week", which is what turns a browser with an ordinary-sized deck into a buyer instead of an admirer.

Everyday · The habit · Widened buyer

Track 03

A sculpture that happens to burn

"The Fennek's designed lines against the house"

The Fennek is a Roderick Vos design first and a fireplace second — clean corten lines that read as an object even before it's lit. I'd treat it the way you'd treat a good piece of furniture indoors: the thing the whole terrace is arranged around, noticed in daylight and not just after dark. That's the story that lets a buyer justify the price to themselves — they aren't buying a stove, they're buying the one designed thing the garden is built around.

Design-led · The object · Justified premium

Track 04

Your idea

"Shaped entirely around you"

The tracks are shaped entirely around you — a specific product line you want to lead with, a season, or an angle of your own. Just say what would lift RB73's communication right now, and the collaboration is built around it.

Bespoke · Your idea · Campaign-ready
